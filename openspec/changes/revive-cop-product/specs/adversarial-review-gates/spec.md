## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Stage transitions require adversarial review

SemOps SHALL require adversarial review before major stage transitions in the COP revival.

#### Scenario: Framework modernization is challenged before implementation

- **WHEN** SemOps is ready to modernize its SemStreams module path, toolchain, and projection contracts
- **THEN** an adversarial review challenges old StreamKit assumptions, framework ownership boundaries, and
  migration blast radius before implementation begins

#### Scenario: COP model is challenged before stabilization

- **WHEN** canonical entities, predicates, ownership claims, provenance, or confidence conventions are proposed
- **THEN** an adversarial review challenges overlapping ownership, product-only vocabulary, and missing upstream
  vocabulary candidates before the model is treated as stable

#### Scenario: Feed enters the stack only after evidence review

- **WHEN** a feed adapter is ready to enter the structural COP stack
- **THEN** an adversarial review challenges parser evidence, replay evidence, compliance wording, mock coverage,
  indexing profile, cardinality risk, and stale-data behavior

### Requirement: High-risk claims require dedicated adversarial review

SemOps SHALL run dedicated adversarial reviews before promoting high-risk product claims.

#### Scenario: Orchestration feature is reviewed before promotion

- **WHEN** orchestration, topology, tier placement, or escalation UI is proposed as a product feature
- **THEN** an adversarial review must show a concrete operator question that the feature answers better than source,
  provenance, and health state alone

#### Scenario: Binary or standards claims are reviewed before promotion

- **WHEN** SAPIENT, KLV/STANAG 4609, streaming binary, or standards-conformance claims are proposed
- **THEN** an adversarial review verifies authoritative fixtures, conformance evidence, memory-bounded behavior, and
  honest claim language before the claim appears in demo materials

### Requirement: Upstream asks require adversarial ownership review

SemOps SHALL review upstream SemStreams asks adversarially before filing them.

#### Scenario: Product pressure is separated from framework need

- **WHEN** SemOps identifies a missing manifest, indexing, provenance, confidence, spatial-temporal, raw-lane, or
  edge/core primitive
- **THEN** an adversarial review records the SemOps workflow, failing test or fixture, rejected product-local option,
  and why the remaining gap belongs in SemStreams

### Requirement: Reviews leave lightweight evidence

Each adversarial review SHALL leave a lightweight review record.

#### Scenario: Review record is auditable

- **WHEN** a review completes
- **THEN** the record includes the decision, objections raised, evidence checked, accepted risks, and follow-up tasks
